How to Play:

🐱 Help the cat avoid cactuses and collect Mexican food!

Arrow Keys (↑↓←→): Move the cat in all directions

SPACE: Start Game / Restart

Each food item gives you +5 points

Survive 20 seconds to advance to the next stage!
